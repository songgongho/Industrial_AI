# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, gender identity, gender expression, sexual orientation, or sexual preference.

We welcome contributions from everyone and are committed to building a community where everyone feels respected, heard, and valued.

---

## Our Standards

**Behaviors that create a positive environment:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get oriented

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment or discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Violent threats or language directed against another person
- Insulting or derogatory comments
- Hate speech or slurs
- Trolling, flaming, or general bad faith
- Doxxing (sharing private information without consent)
- Publishing others' private information
- Spam or commercial promotion
- Other unethical or unprofessional conduct

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by this project, including:
- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Project chats and communication channels
- Project events and conferences
- Any other forums created for project communication

It also applies to public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainer(s):

**Email**: maintainer@example.com  
**Private Report**: [GitHub Private Vulnerability Report](https://github.com/your-username/pcb-lamination-press-defect-prediction/security/advisories)

**In your report, include:**
- Description of the incident
- Who was involved
- When and where it happened
- Any relevant context or links
- How the behavior affected you

All reports will be:
- Taken seriously
- Handled confidentially
- Investigated promptly
- Responded to within 7 days

### Consequences of Violations

The project maintainers have the right and responsibility to:
1. **First violation**: Warning and discussion with the person involved
2. **Repeated violations**: Temporary removal from project spaces (1-30 days)
3. **Severe violations**: Permanent ban from project spaces

The severity and context will be considered in all cases.

---

## Appeals

If you believe a moderation decision was made in error, you may appeal by:
1. Emailing the maintainer with your appeal
2. Providing additional context or information
3. Awaiting a response within 14 days

## Examples

### ✅ Positive Examples
- "I respectfully disagree. Here's my perspective..."
- "I'm new to this project, can someone help me get started?"
- "That's a great idea! I'd like to build on it by..."
- Offering constructive feedback on code reviews
- Helping answer questions from other contributors

### ❌ Negative Examples
- "You're an idiot if you think that code is good"
- "Go back to [ethnicity/place]"
- Asking someone to explain their appearance or identity
- Sharing someone's private information without consent
- Persistent unwanted messages or contact
